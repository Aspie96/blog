The 1st of January is Public Domain Day: the day in which

## The Mickey Mouse Protection Act

The Mickey Mouse Protection Act of 1998 extended copyright terms in the United States to the life of the author plus 70 years or, for works made for hire and anonymous or pseudonymous ones, 95 years from publication or 120 years from creation, whichever is shorter.

Mickey first appeared in 1928 in Steamboat Willie. Accordingly, copyright on the film expires right at the end of 2023 (95 years from publication): on Public Domain Day 2024.

## Mickey outside of the US

Whether Steamboat Willie falls in the public domain in another country will depend on its laws.

According to the [rule of the shorter term](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rule_of_the_shorter_term), the term of copyright protection shall not exceed the term fixed in the country of origin of the work. Accordingly, wherever the rule is followed, Steamboat Willie falls in the public domain.

Not all countries follows the rule of the shorter term. They can also apply it selectively, depending on their agreements with other states.

With some exceptions, European Union members follow the rule of the shorter term towards third countries. In 2023, a German court [applied](https://web-archive-org.translate.goog/web/20110406114300/http://www.lareda.hessenrecht.hessen.de/jportal/portal/t/1v7m/page/bslaredaprod.psml?pid=Dokumentanzeige&showdoccase=1&js_peid=Trefferliste&documentnumber=3&numberofresults=4&fromdoctodoc=yes&doc.id=KORE434782003:juris-r03&doc.part=L&doc.price=0.0&doc.hl=1&_x_tr_sl=de&_x_tr_tl=en&_x_tr_hl=it&_x_tr_pto=wapp#focuspoint) a 1892 agreement between Germany and the United States, concluding that a US work would continue to receive protection under domestic law regardless of whether it was still protected in its country of origin.

Many Italian news outlets have [reported](https://www.open.online/2023/12/24/topolino-charlie-chaplin-opere-scadenza-diritto-autore-2024/) that the first appearance of Mickey Mouse, among other works, would join the public domain in 2024, but they did so with little to no reference to the law. US works are protected under the principle of generic reciprocity. Italian authors' rights law [establishes](https://www.normattiva.it/uri-res/N2Ls?urn:nir:stato:legge:1941-04-22;633~art188) that the duration of protection of foreign works cannot exceed that which they enjoy in their state of origin.
